Approved Product Search

You can search our database for products that have been approved for use on buildings in Miami-Dade County.

To find a particular item, enter the Notice of Acceptance (NOA) number (i.e. 01-0712.04).

Other search options are available to find related types of products. To search for a particular product type, click on Advanced Search. Check one, several or all search parameters (Applicant, Category, Subcategory, Material, Impact Rating and Maximum or Minimum Design Pressure).

To begin your search, click on the "Query" button.

The results of your query will be displayed in a table (best printed in landscape mode).

To start a new search, use your browsers Back button to go back to the search page and click on "Reset" to clear your search parameters.
